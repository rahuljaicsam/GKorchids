More on Product Information

ORPE-D Tablets are formulated to provide relief from acidity, bloating, and stomach discomfort. Pantoprazole helps in reducing the production of stomach acid, while Domperidone enhances gastric emptying and prevents nausea and vomiting. Together, they offer comprehensive gastrointestinal support.

Recommended to be taken before meals as directed by your physician. Regular use under medical supervision can significantly improve quality of life for patients suffering from chronic GERD or related conditions.

FAQ

Q: How should I take ORPE-D Tablets?

A: Take as advised by your doctor, typically before meals. Swallow the tablet whole with water. Do not crush or chew.

Q: Can I use ORPE-D for long periods?

A: Prolonged use should be under the guidance of a physician. Regular check-ups may be required to ensure the medication remains effective and safe.

Q: Are there any side effects?

A: Common side effects may include headache, dry mouth, or mild stomach upset. Consult a doctor if side effects persist or worsen.

Q: Can I take this if I'm pregnant?

A: Consult your doctor if you are pregnant or breastfeeding. They will evaluate the benefits and potential risks before recommending this medication.
