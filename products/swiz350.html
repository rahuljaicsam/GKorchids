More on Product Information

SWIZ-350 Tablets bring together two powerful antifungal agents to combat a wide range of fungal infections. Itraconazole disrupts the fungal cell membrane by inhibiting ergosterol synthesis, while Terbinafine interferes with the fungal enzyme squalene epoxidase, preventing fungal cell growth.

This combination is often prescribed for infections resistant to single-agent therapies, ensuring a more comprehensive approach to fungal eradication. Follow your doctor’s dosage and duration recommendations for the best results.

FAQ

Q: How do I take SWIZ-350 Tablets?

A: Take as prescribed by your healthcare provider, usually once or twice daily. Swallow whole with water, and do not discontinue therapy prematurely.

Q: How long does the treatment last?

A: Treatment duration varies by infection type. Nail infections may need several weeks to months of therapy. Always complete the full course for maximum effectiveness.

Q: Are there any side effects?

A: Possible side effects include headache, nausea, mild stomach upset, or taste changes. If side effects persist or worsen, consult your doctor.

Q: Can I take it with other medications?

A: Inform your doctor of all medications and supplements. They will determine if SWIZ-350 is safe to combine with your current regimen.
