More on Product Information

RIZ-LM Tablets are designed to provide comprehensive relief from both allergy symptoms and mild asthmatic conditions. Levocetirizine reduces the body’s response to allergens, easing sneezing and itching, while Montelukast helps keep airways open by blocking leukotrienes that cause inflammation.

Recommended dosage and duration depend on individual conditions and should be followed as per your doctor’s instructions. Regular use can significantly improve the quality of life for individuals with persistent allergies or mild asthma.

FAQ

Q: How do I take RIZ-LM Tablets?

A: Take the tablets as directed by your doctor, usually once daily. Swallow whole with water, preferably in the evening.

Q: Can I take these tablets long-term?

A: Prolonged use may be recommended based on your condition. Consult your physician regularly for long-term management plans.

Q: Are there any side effects?

A: Mild side effects can include drowsiness, headache, or dry mouth. If side effects persist or worsen, consult a doctor.

Q: Is it safe during pregnancy?

A: Consult your doctor before use if you are pregnant or breastfeeding. The decision will depend on the potential benefits and risks.
