More on Product Information

SWAM-250 Tablets (Terbinafine 250 mg) are indicated for treating a range of fungal infections, including ringworm, athlete’s foot, jock itch, and certain fungal nail infections. By disrupting the cell membrane of the fungus, Terbinafine effectively kills and prevents further growth of the pathogen.

For optimal results, continue the full course of treatment as prescribed, even if symptoms improve earlier. This ensures complete eradication of the fungus and reduces the chance of recurrence.

FAQ

Q: How do I take SWAM-250 Tablets?

A: Take as prescribed by your doctor, typically once daily with or without food. Do not discontinue without consulting your physician.

Q: How long does it take to see results?

A: Improvement can be seen within a few weeks, but nail infections may require several months of therapy. Follow your doctor’s advice for the full duration.

Q: Are there any side effects?

A: Mild side effects may include stomach upset, headache, or taste disturbances. If side effects persist or worsen, consult a healthcare professional.

Q: Can I use this if I’m pregnant?

A: Consult your doctor before use during pregnancy or breastfeeding. The decision will depend on potential benefits and risks.
