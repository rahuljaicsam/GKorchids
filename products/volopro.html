FAQ

Q: How do I apply VOLOPRO?

A: Apply the recommended amount to a clean, dry scalp, massaging gently. Avoid contact with eyes and wash hands after use.

Q: How long before I see results?

A: It may take 3-6 months of regular use to notice visible improvement. Continue application as advised by your doctor.

Q: Can both men and women use it?

A: VOLOPRO is generally suitable for both men and women experiencing early hair thinning. Consult your doctor for personalized advice.

Q: Any side effects?

A: Mild irritation or dryness can occur. If side effects persist or worsen, stop use and consult a healthcare professional.
